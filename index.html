Wild Animals

- Blog about nature -

There are many amazing animals in our world. Here are a few:

Giraffe

Area: Africa

Weight: 900-1200kg

Height: 6m

Interesting Facts:

  • Giraffes have unique spot patterns, like human fingerprints.
  • They can run up to 35 miles per hour over short distances.
  • Giraffes only need about 5 to 30 minutes of sleep in a 24-hour period.

Elephant

Area: Africa, Asia

Weight: 4000-7000kg

Height: 3m

Interesting Facts:

  • Elephants can communicate through infrasound, which humans can't hear.
  • They have excellent memories and can recognize individuals after many years.
  • Elephants use their trunks for drinking, breathing, smelling, and grasping objects.
